# event-actor-create-kill -- Specific Actor (is created or dies)

Triggers when the specified actor is created or killed.

# event-type-create-kill -- Actor of Type (is created or dies)

Triggers when any actor of the specified Actor Type is created or killed.

# event-group-create-kill -- Member of Group (is created or dies)

Triggers when any actor that is a member of the specified Actor Group is created or killed.

# event-actor-screen -- Specific Actor (enters or leaves the scene or screen)

# event-type-screen -- Actor of Type (enters or leaves the scene or screen)

# event-group-screen -- Member of Group (enters or leaves the scene or screen)

Triggers when any actor that is a member of the specified Actor Group enters/leaves the screen/scene.

# event-type-region -- Actor of Type (enters or leaves a region)

# event-group-region -- Member of Group (enters or leaves a region)
